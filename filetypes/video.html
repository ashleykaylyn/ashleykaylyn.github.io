Video Files

AVI

  • Stands for Audio Video Interleave
  • One of the oldest video formats
  • Able to run on Windows, Macintosh, Linux etc.

FLV

  • Stands for Flash Video Format
  • All browsers are compatible with FLV
  • Youtube streams in Flash

MOV

  • Apple QuickTime Movie
  • Developed by Apple
  • Isn't limited to being played on the Mac

WMV

  • Windows Media Video
  • Originally designed for web streaming
  • Mainly used on Windows Media Player

MP4

  • Moving Pictures Expert Group 4
  • Uses seperate compression for audio and video
  • Better than FLV and works with all browsers
